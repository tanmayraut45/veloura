Good to Know

Asked, answered.

How do I book an appointment?

Everything runs on WhatsApp. Message us with your service and preferred day, and we confirm your slot and your artist's name within the hour during opening hours. No app, no login, no hold music.

Do you offer bridal trials?

Yes, and we insist on them for The Veloura Bride and The Trousseau. A trial takes about two hours, two to three weeks before the wedding. You leave with photographs of the look and a written plan for the day.

Do you travel for weddings?

For bridal bookings, yes. We come to your venue or home anywhere in Mumbai at no extra charge; outstation weddings are quoted with travel and stay. All other services are in-atelier only.

Which products do you use?

Professional lines only, chosen for Indian skin and Indian weather. Colour work is ammonia-free, and bridal kits are built on long-wear, camera-tested bases. Tell us about sensitivities when you book and we patch-test first.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reschedule or cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your slot, right there on WhatsApp. Inside 24 hours, we charge half the service value. Bridal advances transfer to a new date but are not refundable.

What are your timings?

Tuesday to Friday, 10 am to 8 pm. Weekends run 9 am to 9 pm and fill about ten days out. We close on Mondays so the team rests and the atelier gets a proper reset.
